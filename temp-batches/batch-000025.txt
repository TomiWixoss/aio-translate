Inventory
You can select multiple things in your Sim's Inventory. Select the Multiselect button to turn it on. You'll know when multiselect is on when the background of the inventory turns green. Now you can select multiple items in the Inventory. Once they are all selected, you can use the buttons on the bottom to either mark every item as Favorite, or Sell every item. Note that you cannot use drag functionality from the Inventory when multiselect is On.
Sims can interact with items in their personal inventories by opening the inventory (click on the panel in the lower right or use the [i] shortcut key) and clicking the item.
You can sort items in your Sims Inventory. You can sort by the name of the objects, the price, or how rare they are, among other things. Use the arrow to the left of the sort interface to sort by ascending or descending.
Sims can put items back into the world by clicking and holding an item to pick it up.\n\nWhile dragging, valid drop locations will be highlighted yellow (such as a refrigerator, or Sims). In addition to being able to place the object directly onto the ground or valid surfaces.
You can filter what you see in the Sim's inventory by using the filter interface. Select the arrow next to the Filter Items interface to display all the filter categories. You can also expand a filter category by selecting the arrow; this provides even more filter options. These sub-filters can be turned on and off for more granularity.
You can mark items in your Sim's Inventory as a Favorite item. Select the item you want to Favorite, and then select Add to Favorites. Favorite Items will be identified with a heart icon in the Inventory, and will filter in their own category.
