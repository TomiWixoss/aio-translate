The Verdant Accord
The lands of the realm shall flourish! Plants will have an increased growth rate. Rarer crystals, metals, fossils and frogs may appear across the land. Fishing is also more bountiful with rarer and larger fish appearing in the waters of the Kingdom!   
The Doctrine of Vitality
All citizens shall be encouraged to embody vigor and discipline! Sims in the Kingdom will increase their Fitness skill over time. Sims in fitness related careers will also have increased Career Performance.  
The Technocrats Mandate
The Crown has a vision for a future guided by intellect and precision. Across the Kingdom, technical related skills will increase, as will technical Career Performance.\n
The Culinary Edict
The crown praises the art of cuisine. Food and Drink across the Kingdom will flourish and be of improved quality. Food-related careers will also enjoy increased Career Performance. 
The Edict of Enlightenment
Knowledge is the backbone of a strong kingdom. Young Sims in the Kingdom will finish homework, school projects and presentations faster. Good grades will be easier to achieve. 
The Artisan’s Proclamation
Let it be known that craftsmanship shall thrive under royal patronage. Crafting skills will increase more. Busking will earn more, and Sims in certain creative careers will enjoy increased Career Performance.   
The Silver Charter
The wisdom of age shall be honored. The realm shall safeguard its elders with dignity and care! Elders can enjoy increased skill gain and a reduced risk of WooHoo-induced overexertion. \n
The Cradle Accord
The Crown calls for a time of prosperity and family! Sims in the Kingdom will have a higher success rate of becoming pregnant and a higher chance of pregnancy resulting in twins or triplets. Toddlers will also have elevated skill gain.
