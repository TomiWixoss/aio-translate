Pine Tree

Graveyard Cypress Tree

American Beech Tree

European Beech Tree
Beech trees require a humid atmosphere and well-drained soil to thrive. They prefer slightly shady conditions and should be given plenty of space to grow.

Also known as the thornapple, whitehorn, or mayhaw, the hawthorne produces masses of small white flowers and small apple-like fruits.

Birch Tree
The birch tree is best-known for its striking white bark dotted with large dark &quot;eyes.&quot; Birches are tolerant of a wide range of conditions and thrive in poor soil.
