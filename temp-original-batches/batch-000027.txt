You can also keep track of your Sim’s Skills in the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Skill panel&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;.\n\nFor more information about specific Skills, check out the Lessons in the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Skills&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; section of the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Live Mode category&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;.
Relationships
Spending time with &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;friends&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; and &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;loved ones&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; is essential to (most) Sim’s happiness. As your Sim builds stronger bonds with those around them, &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;new social interactions&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; and &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;possibilities&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; are unlocked.
&lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Interact&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; with other Sims to build &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Relationships&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; with them. Select the other Sim and pick &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Social Interactions&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; from the appropriate category (select More Choices in the menu to see the categories).\n\nThe &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;outcome&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; of Social Interactions largely depends on your Sim's &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Emotion&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;. The Happy and Confident Emotions are great for making friends, while Flirty can improve your romantic advances.
Open the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Relationship panel&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; to check on your Sim’s Relationships. Under each face in the Relationship panel, there’s a &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;bar&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; displaying your Sim’s &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Relationship score&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; with that Sim. \n\nIf you attempt to romance a Sim, a &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;second bar&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; will eventually appear. Your Romance and Friendship score can be &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;different&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;; for example, Sims can be enemies and lovers at the same time! 
Needs
Your Sim’s &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;mental Needs&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; are &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Fun&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; and &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Social&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;. \n\nInteracting with other &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Sims&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; and using entertainment &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Objects&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; such as a TV, book case, computer, or instrument are great ways to improve these Needs.
Your Sim’s &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;physical Needs&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; are &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Bladder&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;, &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Hunger&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;, &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Energy&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;, and &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Hygiene&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;. \n\nOwning and using a toilet, a kitchen with a fridge and stove, a bed, and a shower or bath tub will help you take care of your Sim’s physical Needs.
If you want to care for your Sim’s well being (or &lt;i&gt;experiment&lt;/i&gt; with it), look no further! \n\nManaging your Sim’s basic &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Needs&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; allows them to live happy, fruitful lives; however, if they get low, they will experience &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;negative Emotions&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; and might even &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;die&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;!\n\nYou can see the current state of your Sim’s Needs in the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Needs panel&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;. If you click on a &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Need icon&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;, your active Sim will try to do something to improve that Need!
Money &amp; Work
One of the best ways to make money is joining a &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Career&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;! Open the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Career panel&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;, select &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Join a Career&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;, and &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;pick one from the list&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; to get one step closer to your first paycheck.\n\nYour Sim will now go to work and earn money! You can see their &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;schedule&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; in the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Career panel&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;.
The key to earning big Simoleons is getting &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;promoted&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;. Your Sim will get promoted when they fulfill their Promotion &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Requirements&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; and max out the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Performance&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; bar. Check out the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Career panel&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; to see your &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;progress&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;.\n\nUsually, Promotion Requirements are reaching certain &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Skill levels&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;. To increase your Performance, your Sim should go to work with a &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;positive Emotion&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; and complete their &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Daily Task&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; before going to work.\n\nYou can read more about Skills and Emotions in other &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Live Mode Lessons&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;.
They say &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;money&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; can’t buy happiness, but it sure helps to keep your Sims content! For example, you can use money to upgrade your &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;house&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; and the &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Objects&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; in it, and pay your &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;bills&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt; to keep the power and water on.\n\nFun fact: the Sim currency is called &lt;b&gt;&lt;font color='#199C2C'&gt;Simoleons&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/b&gt;!
